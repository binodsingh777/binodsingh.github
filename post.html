airline managment system (airline-x)

This was a project I started back in november 2020 when I first learned python. I was the sole creator/developer for the first year of the project before I was able to hire help.

There are two types of users for the Airline Reservation System project. One is the customer and the other is the administrator. Both the customer and administrator user interface

The functional requirements of the Airline Reservation System are divided among the customer and the administrator of the application

Customers had the ability to view all their data with a dynamic search form, trend data, add notes to reports and even map their samples' physical location.
